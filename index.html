The Problem

Hospital environments present significant logistical challenges in hospitalized patient transportation. Manual wheelchair operation consumes valuable staff time and resources — transportation delays can have consequences in emergency scenarios.

The Approach

Flow field pathfinding. Rather than calculating a new path every time the wheelchair moves (like A*, etc...), the algorithm thinks ahead. It maps the entire floor at once, quietly assigning each tile a direction toward the destination.

The Simulation

A simulation system generates continuously transport requests, cycling through Emergency Room, Patient Room, and Cafe in a pattern that's random enough to feel real, but smart enough to never repeat itself.
